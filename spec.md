# Recruiter Hub

## Overview

A recruiting enablement platform and recruiter onboarding platform designed to centralize all training, processes, workflows, videos, and resources for recruiting teams.

## Core Concept

This platform serves as a single source of truth for:

- **Training Materials** - Onboarding curriculum, skill development courses, and certification programs for recruiters
- **Processes** - Standardized recruiting workflows, interview procedures, and candidate evaluation frameworks
- **Workflows** - Automated and guided workflows for common recruiting tasks (sourcing, screening, scheduling, offer management)
- **Videos** - Training videos, recorded sessions, best practice demonstrations, and team knowledge shares
- **Documentation** - Playbooks, templates, scripts, and reference materials

## Goals

1. Accelerate recruiter onboarding and time-to-productivity
2. Ensure consistency in recruiting practices across the organization
3. Provide easy access to up-to-date resources and best practices
4. Enable continuous learning and skill development for recruiting teams
5. Capture and share institutional knowledge

## Target Users

- New recruiters going through onboarding
- Experienced recruiters seeking reference materials
- Recruiting managers building and training teams
- HR/People Ops teams maintaining recruiting standards
